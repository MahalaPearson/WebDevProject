From a beautifully rainy and ever-green state called Washington, I (Mahala Pearson) moved to Utah to attend Brigham Young University. I left my two adorable younger brothers, Sean and Ronan, back home. I moved here to pruse my career in Accounting and continue to serve in the Army National Guard. I participate in one of the Nations best ROTC programs here at BYU, as well as train on a Nationally Ranked Ranger Challenge Team. Since moving to Utah, I have developed my own family on the Ranger Challenge Team, proud to call each one of them my bothers and sisters. I have done and experienced many things in my short life, from being an alter server, a wrestler all the way to producing videos and writting articles for my high school. I take any opportunity given to me with graditude and I firmly believe in no matter what you do putting forth 100 percent effort. I do not believe in wasting the life given to me because of fear or uncertainty.
